## Custom Form Builder

Daycare staff now have access to a new form builder feature. This tool allows you to easily create, edit, and manage your own customized forms directly within VakaCloud.

## More Accurate Care Hour Bookings

The care hour booking system has been further enhanced. The system is now fully timezone-aware and utilizes new validation rules to prevent booking errors, making time management even more reliable.

## Spotted a Missing Translation?

With the introduction of new features, you might still encounter occasional untranslated terms. If you notice any missing translations or anything unusual, please contact our support. Your feedback helps us perfect the service!
